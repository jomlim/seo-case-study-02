About the Business

A family-owned HVAC contractor serving the Miami metropolitan area for nine consecutive years. Their licensed technicians handle residential and light commercial AC repair, system installation, refrigerant recharge, duct cleaning, and seasonal maintenance contracts.

Despite a strong offline reputation built almost entirely through word-of-mouth, the business had never invested in digital marketing. Their website was a basic static page with no SEO, no optimised content, and a mobile PageSpeed score of 31 — effectively disqualified from any competitive search result.

Competitors with fewer years of experience were consistently winning high-value AC jobs online. This engagement was built to fix that.

Challenges

10 Real Obstacles Before Strategy Began

Every challenge shaped the approach. Nothing was templated.

01

Zero Digital Foundation

No GBP, no citations, no on-page SEO — a complete rebuild rather than optimisation of existing assets.

02

Hyper-Competitive Miami Market

National HVAC brands, large regional contractors and heavily optimised local operators all competing for the same keywords.

03

Peak-Season Traffic Being Lost

Miami's extreme summer heat creates massive AC repair surges (May–Sep). Without rankings, every peak-season call went to competitors.

04

Emergency Searches — Hardest to Rank

Terms like emergency AC repair Miami carry the highest conversion intent but are dominated by national home-service platforms.

05

Spanish-Speaking Demographic Missed

Miami-Dade is ~70% Spanish-speaking. The business had no bilingual GBP attributes and zero Spanish-language content.

06

Critical Core Web Vitals Failure

Mobile PageSpeed 31 meant Google's page experience signal actively penalised the site across all target keywords.

07

No Neighbourhood Targeting

The business served all of Miami-Dade — Coral Gables, Hialeah, Doral, Homestead — but one generic page cannot rank for geo-modified searches.

08

NAP Consistency Risk

No prior citation footprint meant any inconsistency introduced during the build could undermine local ranking signals immediately.

09

No Reviews or Social Proof

HVAC 3-Pack listings typically show 50–200+ reviews. The client had zero — a trust deficit in a high-stakes emergency purchase category.

10

AC Terminology Complexity

Miami consumers search across AC unit, air conditioner, HVAC system, mini split, central air — requiring comprehensive keyword mapping to avoid leaving search volume unaddressed.

SEO Strategy

8-Pillar Local SEO System

A structured, multi-layered approach designed specifically for the Miami HVAC market.

📍

1 · Google Business Profile Optimisation

Created and fully optimised the GBP listing: correct NAP, primary category (HVAC Contractor) plus three secondary categories, complete service list with individual descriptions, 750-character business description with primary keywords, 25 professional photos, Q&A section populated with 10 HVAC-specific questions, weekly GBP Posts schedule launched, and bilingual attributes including Se Habla Español. EPA 608 and NATE certification attributes added. Service area extended to cover all Miami-Dade cities.

🔍

2 · Local Keyword Research

Geo-specific research mapped 80+ local search queries across repair, installation, maintenance, and emergency intent clusters. Keywords prioritised by monthly search volume, ranking difficulty, lead intent (transactional vs. informational), and Miami's summer-peak seasonality. Final targeting list: 20 primary + 40 secondary keywords, each assigned to a specific page.

🗺️

3 · 5 City-Specific Local Landing Pages

Designed and developed five high-intent service pages targeting Miami's most valuable HVAC searches. Each page built with: unique H1 and title tag, geo-modified content referencing Miami neighbourhoods and climate, LocalBusiness + Service schema, internal cluster links, transparent CTAs, and meta descriptions optimised for click-through. Pages: HVAC Miami · AC Repair Miami · AC Installation Miami · Emergency AC Repair Miami · AC Maintenance Miami.

📋

4 · Citation Building Campaign — 45 Citations

Structured 45-citation campaign across four tiers: Core platforms (Google, Bing, Apple Maps) → US General directories (Yelp, Yellow Pages, BBB, Angi, HomeAdvisor, Thumbtack) → HVAC trade directories (ACCA, NATE, ASHRAE) → Miami/Florida local directories. Strict NAP consistency enforced. Index verification conducted at campaign close. 44 Live at 90 days.

⚡

5 · Core Web Vitals Remediation

Full PageSpeed audit and phased fix plan: image compression and WebP conversion, render-blocking JS/CSS elimination, server response improvements, LCP element preloaded, CLS sources corrected, INP improved via script deferral. Result: Mobile 31 → 89, Desktop 62 → 96. All three Core Web Vitals now passing "Good" thresholds.

🛠️

6 · Technical SEO Audit & Fixes

Full crawl via Screaming Frog. Fixed: missing and duplicate title tags, missing meta descriptions, broken internal links, missing image alt text, mixed HTTP/HTTPS assets, XML sitemap errors, robots.txt misconfiguration, and non-200 status code pages. Reduced crawl errors from 34 to 1. GA4 and Google Search Console configured with full goal tracking from day one.

🔗

7 · Internal Linking Redesign

Full internal link audit followed by a rebuilt site architecture. Logical content clusters established: homepage → service pages → location pages → blog. Keyword-rich anchor text applied. All landing pages received crawlable links from the homepage and navigation. Orphan pages eliminated. Internal link count grew from 8 to 54 crawlable links.

📣

8 · Local Content & Authority Signals

On-page content optimised for E-E-A-T: Miami neighbourhood references, Miami-Dade climate callouts, Florida DBPR licence number displayed, EPA 608 and NATE certification prominently featured. FAQ schema blocks added across all pages for featured snippet targeting. GBP review response templates created — all incoming reviews responded to within 24 hours. Looker Studio reporting dashboard built and handed over.
